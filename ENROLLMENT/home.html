Application Steps

  1. Step 1: Register or Log In For New Applicants: If you don't have an account, click on REGISTER and provide your basic information to create an account. For Returning Applicants: Log in using your email, username, or mobile number and password.
  2. Step 2: Access the Enrollment Section On the homepage, look for a button or section labeled ENROLL NOW. Click on it to begin the application process.
  3. Step 3: Complete the Online Application Form: Fill out the online form with the following details: Personal Information (Name, Age, Birthdate, etc.), Parent/Guardian Information, Address and Contact Information, Other relevant details specific to the daycare's requirements.
  4. Step 4: Schedule a Free Consultation (Optional) You may book a free consultation to understand the program better. Some centers offer demo sessions.
  5. Step 5: Review and Confirm Your Application Carefully review all the information you have entered. Make any necessary changes before confirming.
  6. Step 6: Await Further Instructions Check your email or account dashboard regularly for updates, including payment information, class start dates, and any additional steps.

Programs

  • Module 1: Early Childhood Development
    • Week 1: Introduction to Early Childhood Development - Understanding the importance of early years.
    • Week 2: Physical Development - Activities to promote gross and fine motor skills.
    • Week 3: Cognitive Development - Engaging activities that stimulate thinking and problem-solving.
    • Week 4: Social and Emotional Development - Building relationships and emotional intelligence through play.
  • Module 2: Health and Nutrition
    • Week 1: Importance of Nutrition - Understanding balanced diets for young children.
    • Week 2: Healthy Eating Habits - Fun ways to encourage healthy eating.
    • Week 3: Hygiene Practices - Teaching children the importance of personal hygiene.
    • Week 4: Physical Activity - Incorporating movement and exercise into daily routines.
  • Module 3: Creative Arts and Expression
    • Week 1: Introduction to Art - Exploring different art materials and techniques.
    • Week 2: Music and Movement - Activities that combine music with physical expression.
    • Week 3: Storytelling and Drama - Encouraging imagination through stories and role-play.
    • Week 4: Cultural Arts - Exploring different cultures through art and crafts.
  • Module 4: Language and Literacy
    • Week 1: Language Development - Activities to enhance vocabulary and communication skills.
    • Week 2: Early Literacy Skills - Introducing letters, sounds, and reading readiness.
    • Week 3: Storytime - The importance of reading and storytelling in early education.
    • Week 4: Writing Skills - Fun activities to develop pre-writing skills.
  • Module 5: Social Skills and Community Awareness
    • Week 1: Understanding Emotions - Identifying and expressing feelings.
    • Week 2: Sharing and Cooperation - Activities that promote teamwork and sharing.
    • Week 3: Community Helpers - Learning about different roles in the community.
    • Week 4: Environmental Awareness - Teaching children about caring for the environment.
